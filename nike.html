about nike

Nike is a famous sportswear brand known for its shoes, clothes, and gear.
It started in 1964 and became Nike in 1971.
Company's logo is the Swoosh, and its slogan is “Just Do It.”
Nike supports top athletes and teams around the world and is known for its cool designs and focus on performance and innovation.
Nike inspires people to push their limits and believe in the power of sport.
